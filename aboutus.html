Our Mission

Pizza Paradise is committed to serving delicious pizzas inspired by the diverse neighborhoods of Chicago. We strive to provide exceptional customer service and create a welcoming atmosphere for all pizza lovers. We would also love to welcome all nearby college students to join us in our pizza paradise lounge. All students are welcome to do homework, hangout, take a pizza break and more. All while enjoying our special student deals.
